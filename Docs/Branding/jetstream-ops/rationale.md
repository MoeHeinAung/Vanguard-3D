# Jetstream Ops — Branding Rationale

> **Tagline Options:** DRAWRING · SALES · SETTLE  |  Jetstream Operations Suite

## Name Breakdown

- **JETSTREAM** — A fast-moving, high-altitude air current. Conveys *speed* (rapid draw processing, real-time sales ingestion) and *reach* (network-wide agent distribution). It is action-forward and memorable.
- **OPS** — Short for Operations; the two-letter suffix locks the wordmark together at the bottom and signals this is a tool for operators, not passive consumers.
- **JETSTREAM.OPS** — The dot-separator reads like a domain/command, reinforcing the console/CLI aesthetic.

The name package — JET ←→ STREAM → OPS — uses the `→` arrow motif (found implicitly in the sweep lines in the mark) to narrate the data flow: *Draw → Sales → Offload → Settlement*.

## Visual Design Choices

### Colour Palette
| Role | Hex | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Background | `#1c1204` | Warm deep charcoal-amber; unique departure from typical "tech blue" palettes |
| Primary Amber | `#f59e0b` | Amber = action, urgency, conditioning; matches lottery-draw energy |
| Hot Red accent | `#ef4444` | Risk and exposure colour; signals the Risk Management module clearly |
| Sunbeam | `#fbbf24` | Lighter amber for speed lines; gives a blazing jet-stream feel |

### Jet-Sweep Mark
The **downward-curving speed arc** (left-to-right) is the literal jet-stream head. A compact section of a racing-style **checkered flag** is baked into the arc terminus — directly evoking lottery drawn number broadcasts and winner announcements. The three **horizontal speed lines** radiate rightward from the tail, communicating: velocity, real-time processing, no lag.

### Wordmark
- **JETSTREAM** — Bold white on amber gradient; the contrast makes it pop immediately. Letter-spacing is tight for its blocky letters, giving mass and speed.
- **OPS_SUITE** — Amber lowercase below; the underscore `_` is a terminal-hacker Easter egg and doubles as a run-of-show divider.

### Supporting Elements
- Terminal horizon lines at y=252/256 subtly nod to the actual beam-summary visuals present in the Vanguard UI.
- Tagline rendered in `Courier New` monospace — evokes operator console output, command logs, and terminal readouts.
- The amber-on-dark palette is warm, distinctive, and does not look like every other dark-mode SaaS product (avoids the "charcoal + neon blue" trap).

## When to Use Jetstream Ops

- Internal operator-focused product naming (draw-room & floor managers speak in active, motion-oriented terms).
- If the product is released as a branded SaaS separate from the legacy "Vanguard" identity.
- Marketing campaigns targeting speed-draw watch-and-bet end-consumers.
- When Risk Management is being positioned as a headline selling point.
